We are Kanish Art

Kanish wood carving group was founded by Mr. Brijesh in the mid -2000. Kanish wood carving created a product line of traditional hardwood mouldings that launched the market for decorative millwork. Their product based on classical rules of balance and proportion, were a refreshing deviation from the paint-grade softwoods that had dominated the market. Building on their success, Mr. Brijesh introduced another division called Kanish Art for printing with the latest technology machine. Kanish group continued to expand their markets with handcarved product collections — Authentic Handcarved and Adornments for Cabinetry and Furniture.

What We Do?

We are the Manufacturer, Wholesaler, Trader & Supplier of Kanish Wood Carving, Mughap 3D, Designer Cupboard, Designer Thumanam, Mughap 2D Design, Grill Board Partition, Crafted Thumanam, etc. To manufacture our products, we are using superior quality of raw material that are procured from our trusted and loyal vendors.

Kanish group also stands apart in delivering services of latest technology printing, like UV Glass printing, printing on materials like Form board, MDF, WPC, ACP, Canvas, Hylam sheet, Forex, Acrylic etc. Our varied day-to-day satisfied customers are the testament of our quality and high technology printing services. With growing demand of our clients for quality products, we stand high in the printing and carving industry in Thrissur and whole of Kerala.

Our infrastructure includes modern machines and equipment that help us to provide smooth finishing to the products. We also have a capacious warehouse which is segregated according to the nature of the products. With our packaging unit, we pack our products in strong materials that help us to transit our products easily and effectively safely.
